3. **RAG knowledge base**: SOPs, PDFs, intranet, ticketing docs

9. **PII redaction + policy controls**
10. **Feedback loop for model improvement**

## Competitiveness analysis (Finland market)

### Why this is competitive
- Most vendors sell generic chatbots; this offer is **operations bot + measurable KPI impact**.
- Confidentiality-first architecture fits regulated verticals (healthcare, finance, utilities).
- Faster implementation cycle (2–4 weeks) with productized scope beats slow custom SI projects.
- Strong ROI story: less support load, fewer delays, better conversion/retention.

### Main competitors
- Local digital consultancies (custom projects, often expensive and slower)
- Enterprise CX platforms (good but expensive, less tailored for Nordic SMB-midmarket)
- In-house IT projects (long cycle, no dedicated AI operations ownership)

### Your positioning
- **“We don’t sell a chatbot. We sell reduced operational friction + faster execution.”**
- Fixed rollout plan, KPI commitments, and governance-grade controls.

## How to maximize close probability (practical playbook)
1. **Account-based outreach**: one custom angle per company (their KPI, not AI hype).
2. **Offer a 14-day pilot** with a clear success metric (e.g., -25% repetitive tickets).
3. **Risk reversal**: fixed implementation fee + month-1 performance checkpoint.
4. **Show governance upfront**: data handling, redaction, auditability, human override.
5. **Use one-page ROI calculator** in every meeting.
6. **Executive + operator dual pitch**:
   - Executive: cost/risk/ROI
   - Operator: workflow speed and less interruption
7. **Land-and-expand**: start with one team (support/ops), then upsell to other units.
