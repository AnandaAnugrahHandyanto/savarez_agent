🧠 Sellable Product Ideas

🛟Support Command Center

One pane for ticket triage, SLA risk, escalation routing and answer suggestions. Best for SaaS + service SMEs with growing support load.

⚙️Ops Control Tower

Brings order exceptions, delayed workflows, and team workload into one dashboard. Position as “waste reduction” instead of “AI experiment”.

💬Client Health Radar

Combines sentiment, churn risk signals and account alerts so CSM/account teams act early. Perfect expansion motion after pilot success.

🚀 30-day GTM Motion

Week 1: Workflow mapping + KPI baseline.
Week 2: Pilot dashboard + alert logic.
Week 3: Team rollout + feedback loop.
Week 4: ROI review + expansion proposal.

💼 Positioning to close

Angle 1 — Cost Relief

  • “Cut repetitive workload by 20–35% in 60 days.”
  • Lead with saved team-hours, not tokens.

Angle 2 — Speed to Resolution

  • “Reduce time-to-resolution by 25–40%.”
  • Promise visibility and routing first.

Angle 3 — Safe AI Adoption

  • Role controls, audit logs, and human escalation.
  • Frames AI as controlled operations layer.
