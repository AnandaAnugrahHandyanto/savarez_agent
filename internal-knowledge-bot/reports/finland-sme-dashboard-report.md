## Competitiveness (why this should sell)
1. **You sell a dashboard workflow**, not a black-box chatbot.
2. **Clear modules**: Leads, Support Triage, Knowledge Answers, Escalations, KPI panel.
3. **Fast deployment**: 2вЂ“4 weeks for first live scope.

## How to maximize close rate
1. Offer a **14-day pilot** with one KPI target (e.g., 20вЂ“30% fewer repetitive tickets).
2. Include **human handoff** and compliance controls from day one.
3. Start narrow: one team + one workflow, then expand.
4. Use weekly performance snapshots: resolved cases, saved time, unanswered intents.
5. Include one-page ROI calc in every proposal.
