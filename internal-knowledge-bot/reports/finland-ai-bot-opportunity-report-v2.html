Universal Feature Stack

  • FI/EN/SV multilingual support
  • RAG from SOPs/policies/docs
  • Role + group access controls
  • Policy engine + human handoff
  • Audit logs + analytics dashboard
  • CRM/helpdesk integrations

How to Win Deals in Finland (Practical)

  • Sell outcomes (ticket reduction, faster resolution), not “AI bot” features.
  • Offer 14-day paid pilot with one KPI commitment.
  • Show governance up front: redaction, escalation, auditability.
  • Run account-specific demos with each company’s terminology.
  • Land one function first (ops/support), then expand to more teams.
