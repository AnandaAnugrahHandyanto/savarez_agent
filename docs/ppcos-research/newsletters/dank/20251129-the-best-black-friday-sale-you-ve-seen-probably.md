Eden is a workspace for creatives. Think if you combined a file storage app, writing app, and canvas app that has AI chats you can connect to visually.
Meaning, you can move all of your creative process there. All of your writing, AI usage, research, video commenting and collaboration, note-taking, and web clipping.
One notable thing that people have been enjoying is the ability to paste a YouTube link and have it downloaded, auto-tagged, and transcribed - so you can quickly reference it with AI or find the exact spot you need. Right in your workspace with all your other files.
There are three core features, among many:
A better place to store your knowledge – Save all of your ideas, files, media, and links in one place. Everything you add to your workspace is automatically transcribed, tagged, downloaded, and ready to reference with AI.
The worlds most powerful visual search – Find any video frame, image, or file – instantly. Describe the b-roll shot to avoid sifting through hours of footage. Search “pink sweater” and Eden will pull up all frames of files that have a pink sweater in them.
Spatial AI for content & research – Close all your AI tools, documents, and research tabs. Add any workspace item – from notes to YouTube videos – and connect them to multiple AI chats, visually.
The rest of the features are on the landing page.
