What is Eden?
It’s a software where you do all of your creative work (your writing, video collaboration, research, outlining, and ideation).
A workspace where you never lose an idea, because how much better would your work be if you never forgot that bookmark, chapter of a book, or educational video? What if you could find exactly what you need, down to the exact video frame?
Sounds cool, I know, but what does that look like?
A drive where things don’t get lost – Save all of your ideas, files, media, and links in one place. Everything you add to your workspace is automatically transcribed, tagged, downloaded, and ready to reference with AI.
The most powerful visual search – Find any video frame, image, or file fast.
Describe the b-roll shot to avoid sifting through hours of footage. Search “pink sweater” and Eden will pull up all frames of files that have a pink sweater in them.
A canvas with spatial AI for content and research – Close all your AI tools, documents, and research tabs. Add any workspace item – from notes to YouTube videos – and connect them to multiple AI chats, visually.
