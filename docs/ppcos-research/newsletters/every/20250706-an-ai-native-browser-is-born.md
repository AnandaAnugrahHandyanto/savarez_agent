Knowledge base
🎧 🖥 "Inside The Browser Company Why They Killed Arc to Build Dia" by Rhea Purohit/AI & I: When The Browser Company decided to stop supporting Arc, fans were devastated, but cofounders Josh Miller and Hursh Agrawal felt they had no choice but to embrace the moment in AI and build something new. Enter Dia. Dan Shipper nabbed the first podcast interview that both founders have given about the transition from Arc to Dia—a compelling story about why sometimes you just need to follow your hunch. 🎧 🖥 Watch on X or YouTube, or listen on Spotify or Apple Podcasts.

"How I Stopped Worrying About AI and Learned to Value My Humanity" by Sari Azout/Thesis: Sari Azout asks a big question: What if we stopped seeing AI as our replacement and started seeing it as “collective intelligence”—or, aggregated human knowledge? Read this if you want to stop panicking about AI and start thinking about what makes work truly “unLLMable.”

"AI Doesn't Care About Your Résumé" by Katie Parrott/Working Overtime: Katie Parrott went from a content marketer to an AI operations lead at Every in just two years—all because she started using ChatGPT. It turns out that the humanities skills she already had were exactly what she needed to thrive with AI. Read this if you want to see how sharing your AI experiments publicly can transform side projects into career opportunities.
