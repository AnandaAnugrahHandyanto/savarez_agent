In 2024, you received 48 Premium editions covering the landscape of
Marketing Strategy, Organization Transformation, and Strategic
Analytics.

For me, that translated into 2024 Gross Revenue of: US $184,100.

All of which was donated to charitable causes.

Over its life of 4.5 years, TMAI has raised US $734,150 for charities.

I'm immensely grateful for your attention, and the fees that you pay
to help make the world a little bit better.

Thank you.

This year, 54% of the funds were donated to causes that directly
helped those impacted by war. Sadly, we saw too much of that this
year.

I continued to direct funds, 38%, to aid early childhood education in
Asia, primarily directed towards education of girls in rural areas.

The remaining funds were directed towards causes advancing civil
liberties.

If your circumstances permit you to, here are three among our
charities who deliver massive ROI: Emergency USA [3], The Marshall
Project [4], and GiveWell [5].

BOTTOM LINE.

I feel immensely lucky that you and I have a relationship where we
exchange knowledge for money. Together, we use our privilege to make
the world a tiny bit better.

Thank you for being a Premium subscriber. Xie Xie. Gracias.
