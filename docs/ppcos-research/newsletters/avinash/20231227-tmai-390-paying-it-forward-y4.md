TMAI #390: PAYING IT FORWARD | Y4

[ Web Version [2] ]

This year was one of tremendous change.

A new job after 16 years, in a vibrant, young company that is doing so
much right, and so much more to do. Shifting the locus of my life to
London. Then New York. Back to London. Rinse and repeat. I can legally
claim my home is not in California!

Through the bigger highs and the smaller highs, I've kept writing this
newsletter. I appreciate that as a signal that I've learned enough new
things to share impactful stories with you each week.

I also immensely grateful for your attention, and subscription fees.

In 2023, TMAI Gross Revenue was $176,000.

All of which was donated to charity.

Over its life of 3.5 years, TMAI has raised $548,920 for charities.

Thank you, so very much.

The focus of our donations continues to be: Civil liberties, early
childhood education, and healthcare.

We've supported education for young refugee children in India, Tibet,
and Africa. A new initiative was Endangered Alphabets [3], to support
the revival of Mende Kikakui script.

We've continued to support EMERGENCY's project
[4] in Sudan, focusing on neo-natal care for the underprivileged,
and healthcare projects in the Middle East.

We added a meaningful donation to Mother Jones magazine [5], to
bolster their coverage of Civil Liberties in America, and Equal
Citizens [6] to bring equal representation for all citizens for the
United States.

I invite you to consider supporting the organizations above.

BOTTOM LINE.

I feel immensely lucky that you and I have a relationship where we
exchange knowledge for money. Together, we use our privilege to make
the world a tiny bit better.

Thank you for being a Premium subscriber. Xie Xie. Gracias.

Sending you good vibes for a joy-filled 2024.

-Avinash.
