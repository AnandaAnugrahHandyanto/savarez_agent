TMAI #420: UNLOCK AI-POWERED PPC!

[ Web Version [2] ]

Change is hard.

Most of us get comfortable managing our responsibilities in Marketing
a certain way, and then we stick to those patterns. 25% because a bit
of laziness sets in. 75% because we experimented, we trial and
_errored_, and those things we are comfortable with worked.

In TMAI #413 [3] we explored how we are on the cusp of a massive
revolution with AI Search (Bing or Perplexity or even Google). That
was a jolt to wake up and prevent what could be a bankruptcy level
event for some businesses.

In a similar vein, I’ve been nudging our clients to take advantage
of features and possibilities enabled by Machine Learning when it
comes to Paid Search campaigns. At times, it is hard. PPC has been
around for so long, we can get a bit too comfortable with the past.

My peers and I have identified a set of PPC solutions that deliver
non-normal results. I thought I would share that list with you – _as
hey, are we doing this, why don’t you rush and also activate these
proven winners!_

1. MAKE AI SMARTER: IMPLEMENT EC & CM.

A Machine Learning algorithm without good data is a tiger without
feet.

This is exponentially true about conversion data, because we want
these algorithms to learn from outcomes on our website/app.

ENHANCED CONVERSIONS (EC) collects additional data from the User when
they convert. This data assesses past User touchpoints to _claw back_
up to 80% of the lost conversions, and, crucially, attributes it to
the correct source. Appealing no?

Consent Management platforms, _would you like cookies with your
burger_, ensure compliance with privacy and data protection laws. For
the subset of users who decline first-party cookie tracking, (the only
one that really matters, 3P cookies are close to useless, do not
bemoan their death), CONSENT MODE (CM) will adapt the Google tags and
use intelligence to model the lost conversion attribution. Done right,
CM can claw back approx. 60% of conversions and attribute it to the
correct source.

Even in the best case, we will lose correct source attribution for
some of our conversions. But, losing 30% is much worse than losing 9%.

[Premium member bonus: ]

#2. WRITE BETTER PRODUCT HEADLINES & DESCRIPTIONS.

Your product feeds power shopping placements (YouTube, Demand
Gen/Discovery etc.). These feeds can account for a significant chunk
of PPC revenue for retailers.

If you provide high-quality feeds, your performance via Pmax’s IA
powered magic will significantly improve across Search, Display, and
Discovery because Google will understand each product you sell better.

That in turns means better targeting of User intent with your ads,
improving ad attributes like titles and descriptions (with a cascading
positive impact on your Quality Score etc.).

That in turn, loads of turns (!), will cluster real-time bidding
strategies to customize individual product performance – it is not a
stretch to see how that improves bid efficiency.

You know my obsession with Profit.

The_ cost_of_goods_sold_ field in your feeds allows you to push margin
data through SA360, instantly creating an opportunity for your
team/agency to maximize profit instead of maximizing revenue. Has your
Agency helped you take advantage of this killer feature? Do you know
your POAS [4]?

#3. BROAD MATCH FTW!

When I was a baby, Broad Match was death.

My fav example: A business selling night stands for bedrooms and used
Broad Match. They burned through a ton of budget, high bounces, before
realizing the ads were being targeted to “_one night stand_”
queries. They did not anticipate humans having high interest in that
query.

Exact and Phrase Match only from that day on. :)

We live in opposite world now.

The combination of _natural language processing_ (NLP) and _large
language models_ (LLMs) mean that Search engines are ten million times
better at understanding the User’s intent – regardless of the
words the human typed into Bing.

You can have fewer keywords in your account (48 instead of 589), spend
just about the same (10,751 instead of 10,360) and get significantly
more Conversions (748 instead of 585!).

Google recently gave the profoundly unsexy moniker Power Pair to Pmax
+ Broad Match. Overlook the unappealing name, activate the immense
value that's inherent in that combo.

I’ll repeat this below: If you are an old AdWords warrior like me,
resist the urge to micromanage (ex: with negative keywords). Sure,
don’t pick ambiguous single keywords, sure put budget caps. But,
subsequently, trust that the AI can find the intent better than your
micromanagement. Significantly better.

To ensure optimal smarts, pair the Power Pair strategy with tactics
above and below (essentially smart bidding). This way, you trust the
Search Engine to do what it does best, but you 100% control what
success is. Win-Win.

#4. GET CLOSER TO HUMAN-CENTRIC MARKETING.

For the last 500 years, you’ve been able to slap the Facebook or
Google Conversion tag on your website, and the platforms would use
that to send you _more likely to convert_ traffic.

If you buy my Macro AND Micro Conversion optimization recommendation
(and you should!), you could pass Micro Conversions and deliver a
broader set of business outcomes.

Pre-AI, this was fantastic, but not magnificently glorious because the
scale at which old school math could figure out the best bid,
targeting, creative was limited.

Today, the scale at which algorithms at Meta, Baidu, others are able
to learn, across a literal tens of billions of data points, is more
than a little mind-blowing.

What do they need?

Some more data. :)

More 1P data to realize that both Joon and I converted, but Joon was
worth $680, and I was $210.

And, Joon was an existing customer, having placed 14 orders all over
$500, and I had done so just twice (in four years!), both below $250.

And, behavioral data for the Joons, Avinashs, Xixis, Sergios, and
others to extract signals that can be merged with data ad platforms
have (tens of thousands of additional signals), to better target ads,
bids, creatives.

And, data that identifies the type of customer you consistently lose
money on.

And… As far as you are comfortable in the quest to double, triple,
quadruple the incremental profit from your paid media.

Features you will activate will sound like: Conversion Value Rules,
Upweighting big strategies, Propensity Modeling, Value-Based Bidding,
Offline Conversion Import, etc. Check how many of those you are
currently using.

With every step above, you might have noticed we are going away from
the old _keyword-centric_ marketing or even_ we are getting some
conversions-centric_ marketing, and on to _human-centric_ marketing.
It is still not 1:1, but it is getting very, very good at _tiny
segments of intent-centric_ marketing – only a handful of steps away
from fully human-centric marketing.

[NOTE: To take full advantage of AI this hard thing needs
accomplishing: Don’t micromanage, don’t try to understand
everything, let the Ad Platform control the _HOW_ it does things. You
fully control _WHAT_ success is. As long the success you want is being
delivered, in the quantity you expect, trust that the algorithm, in
this narrow instance, is smarter than you’ll ever be, in this narrow
context.]

#5. BUILD HIGH-PERFORMANCE CREATIVE.

If I’ve wormed on thing into your brain with TMAI, it is this one:

55% - 70% OF THE SUCCESS OF ALL MARKETING IS THE CREATIVE.
It is.

If GenAI has done one thing that falls in the non-hype category, it is
to radically improve the creative that goes into your display or video
ads.

A year ago, I would still have recommended using our own tools, other
third-party tools and vendors, your own employees, to build better
creative executions. Today, I want you to save all that money, people,
vendor tax, and ever increasing complexity, and hand it all over to
the GenAI options at Meta and other Ad Platforms. They are so much
better at this, they have so much more data to learn from. (And, the
"GenAI Creative" vendors trying to badmouth the Ad Platforms are using
the root LLMs from the same platforms!)

Upload a few product shots, see them come to life in 10,000
executions, see that whittled down to the 23 that are driving the
highest value conversions (how? see #3 above).

Upload a full length three-minute brand movie that is of nearly no use
on any ad platform (and delivers no revenue/brand lift), see that
transformed into a ton of variations in short-form formats, see that
drive brand lift/revenue!

I understand it is hard to trust the platform, old habits die hard.
Your CMO loves a very, very specific shade of Purple. I hope it will
help reduce hurt feelings when she sees a massive increase in
Conversions/Intent.

If someone in your company is not actively feeding everything
Meta/Google needs to build better creative for you…. It might be the
single biggest mistake you are making to prevent business success.

REMEMBER: You still own Creative Concepts, path above is for your
Creative Executions. Additionally, You also still own identifying that
success is – and ensure you are feeding that into the platform.

How do you know your agency/team is on this, you’ll start to hear
phrases like Responsive Search Ads, Dynamic Search Ads, and it goes
from there.

BOTTOM LINE.

I grew up in a world where Ad Platforms were fairly “dumb.” They
played cheap tricks to get you Clicks, or tricked you into believing
lame things like_ View-Thru Conversions_. My Marketing strategy and
tactics worked around those limitations/lameness.

Martin reports to me, he is a generation younger than me (ouch!), he's
grown up in an entirely new reality.

To prevent being irrelevant, I try to be like Martin: Comfortable with
massively more intelligence in ad platforms and analytics solutions,
leveraging automation to the max possible to achieve scale, and
optimizing everything through the lens of _trust them, but verify
results._

Be more like Martin.

Carpe diem!
