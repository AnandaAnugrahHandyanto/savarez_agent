TMAI #440: WILL THE REAL SLIM ROI, PLEASE STAND UP? -P2

[ Web Version [2] ]

In part one, we reflected on the four different formulas [3] we can
use to from _fake news _ROI (1) to _real news _ROI (3) to _really
real new _ROI (4).

From Revenue ROI 1 formula of:

ROI = [(REVENUE – MEDIA COSTS)/(MEDIA COSTS)]
To Incremental Net Profit ROI 4 formula of:

ROI 4 = [(IREVENUE – ICOGS – INON-WORKING COSTS – MEDIA
COSTS)/(CAMPAIGN BUDGET)]
The exercise allowed us to illuminate this, frankly a little sad,
picture of our advertising campaign's business impact:

[Four ROI computations, which one are you using?]

Realizing our _real _ROI is -0.7 while sad, is helpful. And, welcome.
It empowers you and me to sit down with the truth, and find a path to
better.

[Eagle-eyed among you will note a change to ROI 3's formula, vs. part
one. I'm grateful to Matthias for spotting an error. I've corrected it
above.]

[SPECIAL NOTE: The ROI computations above spans a four to six month
_impact horizon_. For brand marketing campaigns, the _impact
horizon, _will stretch beyond six months. For these types of
campaigns, we compute short-term ROI 4 using different KPIs (# People
Lifted, Cost Per Individual Lifted - both vs. baselines), and
different methodologies (true test-control surveys, not pre-post). For
these brand marketing campaigns, we measure long-term ROI 4 with the
same KPIs (incremental Profit), but different methodologies, (longer
_impact horizon _like _advanced attribution modeling_, _ML-based_
_mix models, _and _CausalAI_).]

Deep breath.

RISING LIKE A PHOENIX!

Our ROI lays burnt in a pile of ashes, at -0.7.

It is ok. Knowing is good.

We can start asking: _How can we get better?_

You are going to sit down with your super clever Marketing team.
Brainstorm. You will inspire (/force) your Performance Agency to raise
to meet higher standards. You will work with your Product and
Engineering teams. You will lock down your web experience team and
request/beg them to think bigger.

YES!

Time for action.

THE INTERNAL TEAMS RISE.

The four internal company teams responsible for 37% (O M G!) of the
campaign budget going to _non-working costs_ get together and figure
out how to be better.

They figure out how to continue to use creative that has not come
close to getting to _wear-out, _instantly dropping the entire raft of
Creative Development Costs dramatically.

The "Research & Analysis" team drops two _nice to have _measurement
initiatives that had never resulted in anything actionable.

The campaign team is trimmed to just the essentials.

Finally, of the four agencies involved with variable results, just two
are used to deliver this campaign and the other two agencies find
other good clients.

Net net... _Non working media _costs cut by 17 points! And, a
material increase in _working media_ that can be dedicated to
delivering better results.

Outcome...

[4 ROIs | Reducing Non-Working Media Costs.]

All else being equal, the crucial Net Profit ROI 3 goes from negative
to positive: 0.2 vs. -0.1.

On an incrementality basis, a modest improvement from -0.7 to -0.6.

Clear, measurable progress to be proud of.

THE PERFORMANCE AGENCY RISES.

You have a super smart Performance Agency, they will be deeply
troubled that their efforts are falling short (they own ROI 3, ROI 4).

They are going to deploy the complete Incrementality Tech Stack
[4], and, having identified actionable insights, proceed immediately
to making dramatic changes to the strategy and tactics they are
deploying on your behalf for this campaign (and all future ones).

Among actions your Performance Agency takes to improve incrementality:

Dramatic reduction of bids on Brand Keywords.

Deployment of an integrated with ad platforms Propensity Model
[5] that in real-time identifies 2x LTV likelihood prospects.

Deployment of the Power Pair on Google.

Smart Bidding and Smart Creative features on Meta.

Incorporating wear-out rules for creative on TikTok.

Cutting Display Advertising budgets that prove minor or zero
incremental impact.

Cut TV budgets by 20%, and redeploying it to CTV (with a big increase
in impact, and zero loss of TV impact).

And, a hundred more possibilities that boost incrementality.
This transformation will change the incrementality of your advertising
efforts from 30% to 95%!!

Outcome…

[4 ROIs | Agency focused on incrementality. :)]

Fantastic outcome!

The super difficult ROI 4 went from -0.7 to just +0.1!

On an incrementality basis, for just the Agency’s first time, this
is remarkable.

Remember, every dollar we brought in on an incrementality basis, the
company would not have gotten without advertising.

We actually added positive value to the business. Yea!

[Note: For CFOs on TMAI: On an Opportunity Cost basis, this does not
look so good. But, it is a start. And, don't forget us Marketers are
no longer fake newsing_ _you with _ROI IS +4!_]

THE WEBSITE TEAM RISES.

We have always known that the website is the Achilles heel of your
existence.

Your CMO finally puts her foot down, and redirects the IT teams, the
UX teams to focus on the B (behavior) in the ABO framework [6].

Challenged, they change the focus on 40 small A/B tests, the focus on
small tweaks to marginal pages/buttons, the wasted resources on a
hundred research projects.

They launch a big bang beautiful experience that solves for
both _help me make up my mind what I really need, _and the _I'm
here just to place an order, please make it easy _use cases.

They shift a focus from just the checkout or the 2.5% Conversion Rate
users, to the much larger pool of users who form the 35% Micro
Outcomes Rate. This requires entire new experiences, a focus on
medium-term success (atleast) and long-term success (ideally). It
means building content that ensures more of the 35 end up in the 2.5
in the near-future. And, so much more in this spirit.

Impact…

[4 ROIs | Impact from website experience improvement.]

A near doubling of the Conversion Rate!

Finally.

Even with the most difficult ROI computation God has given us, iROI we
see an amazing 1.1!

iROI will never be 8 or 10 or even 5. That is ok. There are only so
many net new _only driven by Marketing _customers out there. Celebrate
the 1.1 (and then work on nudging it higher).

There is no question at this point, across the board, that the CMO and
the CFO are proud of the results Marketing is delivering.

Let’s close by pulling in the rest of the company.

The Engineering Team Rises.

Companies tend to operate in silos.

You are a silo breaker!

You go to your Engineering team and challenge them to consider
improvements to the product, the production process, logistics and
everything else that goes into the Costs of Goods Sold.

They are super brilliant, and they deliver an astounding twenty point
improvement.

Outcome…

[4 ROIs | Impact from reduction of COGS.]

BOOM!

: )

Let me remind you one more time:

A 2.4 ROI on an incrementality basis will be felt in Market Share
increases.

Obviously, we want even more, but I just wanted you to know even this
level will be a competitive advantage.

What an incredible journey!

Ask for formulas of the KPIs you report.

Investigate the methodology powering the formula.

Count all the costs it takes to put one ad in front of one customer.

All Gods have identified incrementality is the destination.

Improving Advertising’s ROI is everyone’s job – not just your
Agency. Create conditions where everyone in the company can pitch in
to deliver incremental results.
