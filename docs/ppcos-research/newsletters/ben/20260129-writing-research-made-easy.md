Hey folks,
Prism by OpenAI [ https://substack.com/redirect/337d4f31-fa7a-4187-8153-097b14a50577?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ] - It’s a LaTeX editor for scientists to draft/work on research papers with AI. Free for ChatGPT users, with unlimited projects and collaborators. It has an editor that both you and GPT-5.2 can edit, and a preview that renders the LaTeX input. Read more on their blog [ https://substack.com/redirect/870bc2dc-d0b2-4d32-b30d-11da16126f13?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ]. Also, Prism’s founder went from idea to OpenAI in less than 16 months [ https://substack.com/redirect/f8f99f20-0421-4d57-814b-b0f3d0b5a7e6?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ].
Two thoughts on this:
Last year, the first release from OpenAI was Operator - a web browsing agent, and we saw dozens of variations of it in 2025. This year might be document editors.
AI for developers is a relatively solved problem for OpenAI. Their next “frontier” target audience is definitely scientists with plans to build an AI researcher intern [ https://substack.com/redirect/7485027f-55cc-4840-aeb1-509df34dcbad?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ] by Sept 2026.
— Keshav [ https://substack.com/redirect/ac8851b6-d3c1-4550-835d-2b95759875d3?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ]
Related: OpenAI’s CFO, Sarah Friar, recently talked about [ https://substack.com/redirect/d2042558-02a2-40fb-b909-51561a372baf?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ] licensing OpenAI models to companies in a way that if they contribute to a discovery, OpenAI can get a piece of the pie. (more clarifications [ https://substack.com/redirect/b189044d-cf44-4dad-ba4d-cfe10d7f2c58?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ])
Gemini 3 Flash can now plan how to analyse an image and use tools to zoom in and annotate it for better visual understanding. It increases performance on visual benchmarks by 5-10%. Google calls it “Agentic Vision [ https://substack.com/redirect/a71dce6b-496e-436b-9473-bbd1515a38de?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ]”, though OpenAI’s models have had this since o3.
Chrome is also becoming an AI browser [ https://substack.com/redirect/20f5a9eb-c506-4001-83bb-80c32c0ec64f?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ]. The Gemini integration now opens a sidebar with features like image generation, access to other Google tools, and auto-browsing. Only in the US for now.
Give your AI the power to listen. Gladia [ https://substack.com/redirect/88133a9f-7605-4164-95bb-db57ccbeb262?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ] is a speech-to-text API that turns real conversations into structured data for agents, workflows, and automation. ~300ms latency, 94% accuracy, and a lightweight SDK lets you add production-grade voice in minutes. Test real-time or async in our playground. [ https://substack.com/redirect/88133a9f-7605-4164-95bb-db57ccbeb262?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ]*
