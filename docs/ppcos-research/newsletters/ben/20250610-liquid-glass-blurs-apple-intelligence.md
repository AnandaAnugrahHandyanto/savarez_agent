BUT it all comes down to if the local models are good enough [ https://substack.com/redirect/bb9aa35d-5852-4fb3-9547-3b2fce34b913?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ]. and that’s where developers come in, these models will be available via the “Foundation Models [ https://substack.com/redirect/cb805096-5938-46ae-b765-0a802012445f?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ]” framework.
