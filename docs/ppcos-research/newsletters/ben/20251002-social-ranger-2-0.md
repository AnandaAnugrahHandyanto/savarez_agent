Hey folks, for the entire day, I was wondering, does anything other than Sora even deserve to be here? OpenAI released Sora 2 [ https://substack.com/redirect/66f36922-4f57-4edb-b5ee-8b4215e3fea6?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ], with a new social media app called…you guessed it, Sora [ https://substack.com/redirect/bd17ba0c-10d7-43ca-aebe-c294bfbcdf51?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ].
The app has a TikTok-like feed—with videos created by Sora. These videos have a) sound (just like Veo 3) and b) ‘real’ people. The addition of people is the killer feature (called Cameo), where anyone can create a short video of themselves saying a few things, moving their head around and then allow friends (or anyone) to add them to a Sora video.
It’s invite-only and only available in the US and Canada for now, but you can watch the videos, like Sam Altman and OpenAI employees launching Sora [ https://substack.com/redirect/b5c23926-f765-4c2a-9a97-c203b0c7bbfb?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ], or Pikachu performing an EDM set [ https://substack.com/redirect/b98263e3-68ea-4b3e-8464-1bfcac8cd03e?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ], anime Jesus [ https://substack.com/redirect/258001e6-d82a-4d66-99b8-941f92541539?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ], or the hidden Steve Ballmer inside Sam Altman [ https://substack.com/redirect/44a77c1d-8e6b-4e61-88b5-273bca8ba559?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ].
Another cool upgrade in Sora 2, beyond realism, audio and Cameo, is the movement of characters. See this video of a skateboard flip [ https://substack.com/redirect/4c32cbd7-1274-46e1-9aeb-b52417672925?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ], or Theo running in an alley [ https://substack.com/redirect/e460713e-466e-4139-b8b9-08a709c388f4?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ]. No sudden jumps or appearing out of nowhere.
Meta also launched a feed of AI-generated videos last week, called Vibes, but the vibes (pun intended) in Sora are much less towards AI-slop, especially because a) the OpenAI team is working hard pushing out funny videos with real people (mostly Sam) and b) the product is much better (yep, I scrolled both feeds for 20-30 mins each).
I am certainly on the ‘no more AI slop’ side of the table with these products - but I’m also aware of ‘no dick pics’ moment of Snapchat, or similar tales that all of a sudden were just part of daily life (in a big way - TikTok started as Musically that was like awful lip-syncs and dances, which still happen on TT today 😅). I guess it’s easy to look at slop as slop now, but I wouldn’t set up camp comfortably on any side; things change a lot.
Thinking Machine Labs (the company by ex-CTO of OpenAI, Mira Murati) released their first product: Tinker. Tinker is a fine-tuning API [ https://substack.com/redirect/1ea30cae-dc47-4c99-94e1-91ecbce1b154?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ], built for researchers who want to work with large (>10B) models, test their own algorithm and data, but don’t want to deal with the infra. The API exposes low-level primitives like forward_backward & sample and focuses on LoRA for fine-tuning (vs RFT). (more details [ https://substack.com/redirect/6d07710c-ca01-4a2e-853c-e11b3a81ecd9?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ])
Claude is now available in Slack [ https://substack.com/redirect/70463f5c-6120-48f2-a3be-9ea5d3b11f89?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ], and the “Imagine with Claude [ https://substack.com/redirect/a41599f6-a946-4621-a1fd-d8793cb60f72?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ]” demo is now open for Pro subscribers ($20/mo plan) as well.
