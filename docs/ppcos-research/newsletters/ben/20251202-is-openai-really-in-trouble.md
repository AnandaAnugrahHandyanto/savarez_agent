Hey folks,
The Information reports Sam Altman declared “Code Red” [ https://substack.com/redirect/77c86e2f-7b13-4446-88c3-834458b3d62e?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ] inside OpenAI after Gemini 3’s success. Ads in ChatGPT will be delayed, with the same fate for products like Agents and Pulse. Personalisation, image generation and model behaviour will be the main focus. The report also claims that OpenAI will release a new reasoning model better than Gemini 3 next week.
DeepSeek’s new models, DeepSeek V3.2 base and Speciale [ https://substack.com/redirect/463f843e-bb52-4d81-a346-24aa15fbff18?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ], are impressive (and open) BUT not better than any of the other top models (GPT-5.1/Codex, Opus/Sonnet 4.5, Gemini 3). The Speciale version can get a gold medal in math olympiads, but it’s only good for proving theorems, it seems, and you can think of the base version as almost the same level as GPT-5 though you’ll see subpar results in both use cases: chat and coding.
FLUX models by Black Forest Labs were the original stable diffusion killer. With FLUX.2 family of image generation models [ https://substack.com/redirect/7444cef0-2979-40bb-9f08-7d3081f1cc51?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ], their next goal is Nano Banana and dozens of open models from China. FLUX.2 [dev] is the 32B open weights variant, better and 3x cheaper than the original Nano Banana (10x cheaper than Nano Banana Pro). BFL also raised a $300M Series B. [ https://substack.com/redirect/d9540631-da41-4144-8d9f-9d92b48f9dea?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ]
The leading video generation model swapped hands by a significant margin. Runway is the new leader with its latest Runway Gen 4.5 model [ https://substack.com/redirect/9acfe5cc-b295-413f-9984-40e0ea2d26c9?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ], beating Google’s Veo 3. Kling, a close competitor, also released a new model, Kling O1 [ https://substack.com/redirect/8bfb2a09-7582-4c96-9f6d-9ec481e1c5cc?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ], exclusively on Fal, so it’s not on the leaderboards yet.
