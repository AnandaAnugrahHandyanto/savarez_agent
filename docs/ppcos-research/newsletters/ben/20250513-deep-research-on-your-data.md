OpenAI Updates:
Health Bench [ https://substack.com/redirect/3af5adcb-4a60-443b-b011-b4aa45be9695?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ] - a new benchmark that tests how good the models are at flagging emergency situations, doing data work in healthcare, accurate communication and more. o3 does best, grok is #2 with gemini 2.5 pro at almost the same level.

This guide from OpenAI has use cases on how companies are using RFT [ https://substack.com/redirect/17a5de52-6e53-45d1-a44a-effabcbfb15d?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ].

AssemblyAI just dropped a prompt-based Speech Language Model [ https://substack.com/redirect/be7b688a-b7e1-4d1a-bcdb-9175d063fc26?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ] that boosts transcription accuracy with zero retraining. Now, AssemblyAI offers Speech AI models that transcribe and understand conversations better than ever, making it easy to build voice-powered apps. Start for free [ https://substack.com/redirect/664500d5-e1b5-4972-a6fe-4ec4c80e2a3b?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ] and ship faster.*
Google's pushing what Gemini can understand from videos [ https://substack.com/redirect/b680f853-c91b-427d-9545-fa859edd08b8?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ]. Gemini models from day 1 are capable of that. Technically, it's not “video” but images (sampled at 1 frame per second) + audio interleaved together. Previously a) the performance was not that good and b) you had to do the work of passing the audio, images, etc. on your own as a developer. Since late 2024 gemini has the ability to accept raw video directly from their SDK and the new Gemini 2.5 models are really good so that 1fps is enough for most tasks.
