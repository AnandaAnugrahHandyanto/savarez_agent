Hey folks,
Google Labs products have been on a spree of launches: Scheduled Tasks [ https://substack.com/redirect/9f671afc-678c-49e1-87ce-a20cce44cc44?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ] and proactive suggestions [ https://substack.com/redirect/49fee3d2-94c4-4dba-8886-2e818c625655?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ] in Jules, their async coding agent. Pomelli [ https://substack.com/redirect/a57f3da0-c517-4c04-8047-5c89e74d634a?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ], their marketing asset creation tool, now lets you animate those generations. And some more in Stitch, their design exploration tool [ https://substack.com/redirect/d7c43cdc-e400-4b21-bb3e-e7669af86383?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ].
Mistral is joining the CLI squad with their open-source entry, Mistral Vibe [ https://substack.com/redirect/7f4dbc0c-4110-4c0d-9b60-d22322f0eb1e?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ]. Its license allows you to fork/build on top of it, and it’s pretty minimal, so you can add your own take without fighting against the existing codebase (looking at you Gemini CLI).
btw, did you see? droid is the top-performing agent [ https://substack.com/redirect/07523ed1-53bd-4941-9a1e-be3c448c8c64?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ] on terminal bench, again. really cementing itself.
sorry, I digress; back to Mistral.
Along with the CLI, it released two more open-source models: Devstral 2 and Devstral 2 Small [ https://substack.com/redirect/94c04dd5-539a-47de-baf9-f3f6a8b70148?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ]. They perform better than GLM 4.6 and Kimi K2, and are on par with DeepSeek 3.2 while being much smaller than them. But these models are still chunky and meant for business with a few GPUs. You’ll have trouble running even the smaller one on a MacBook Air.
Shopify launched a SimGym - Digital customers that behave like real ones. They browse your site, complete tasks, and reveal optimisation spots. You can even run A/B tests with zero live traffic. It’s part of their winter launch [ https://substack.com/redirect/167f238f-f84f-4e44-a96d-cae7cc8b126a?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ] with more AI features.
OpenAI posted a blog claiming their new models are now reaching a “high” level of cybersecurity capabilities [ https://substack.com/redirect/55fbfa3b-caae-4f8d-bf93-d3852ed10525?j=eyJ1IjoicXd0ciJ9.0tEpqnOrcfuKYRYnEOurmlOZ_pVIUnS06konjQA-bCs ] with GPT-5.1-Codex-Max already scoring 76% (vs 27% for GPT-5 in Aug).
