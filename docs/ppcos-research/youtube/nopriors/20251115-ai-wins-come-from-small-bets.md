## Summary

The speaker suggests that companies should not obsess over immediate large returns on investment (ROI) when exploring AI technologies. Instead, they recommend taking small, incremental steps to understand and utilize AI tools like ChatGPT for everyday tasks, which can help demystify the technology and increase the chances of finding valuable applications. The key is to run multiple quick projects to get a feel for the changing landscape of AI.
