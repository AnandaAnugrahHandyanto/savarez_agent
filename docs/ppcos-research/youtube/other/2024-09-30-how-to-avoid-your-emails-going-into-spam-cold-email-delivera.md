## Summary

To improve cold email deliverability and avoid spam filters, focus on sending highly relevant offers from multiple email accounts with low volume, while also ensuring a legitimate online presence and varied sending patterns. Prioritize the "HERO" framework: High-impact offer, Expensive horizontal scale, Rich variety, and Outstanding reputation, rather than relying on gimmicks like avoiding open rate tracking.
