## Summary

To avoid the "intelligence curse" where AI benefits a few at the expense of many, invest in open-source AI to commoditize intelligence and design AI systems that empower users while preserving their data control. Individuals should also protect their knowledge, pursue unique career paths, and embrace ambitious projects to remain relevant in an AI-driven economy.
