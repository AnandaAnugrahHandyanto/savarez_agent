## Summary

The speaker describes a rapid product development process where they come up with a functional prototype in the morning, recruit potential users, and ship the product to them in a separate Slack workspace. By the afternoon, they are running a full-scale experiment, observing users' interactions and struggles, and by the evening or next day, they review and identify areas for improvement.
