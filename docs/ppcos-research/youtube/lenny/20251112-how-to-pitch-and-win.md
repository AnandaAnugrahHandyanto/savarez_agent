## Summary

Here's a 2-3 sentence summary:

When selling to leaders, founders should focus on vision casting and selling opportunities rather than getting bogged down in specific problem details. The key is to show potential future impact, similar to showcasing "Mario on Blast" instead of just the power-up mushroom, which excites top-tier customers and motivates them to take action. Additionally, having the founder directly involved in the sales process is crucial, as people are naturally drawn to engaging with company founders.
