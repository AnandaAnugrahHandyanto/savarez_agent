# style_prompt_contract.md

## 모델 정책
- primary_model: fal-ai/gpt-image-2
- fallback_model: FLUX

## text_policy
- integrated_bubbles: true
- integrated_captions: true
- language: ko
- max_text_elements_per_panel: 2

## style_positive
- mainstream Korean teen webtoon aesthetic
- clean Naver-style school drama lineart
- polished digital manhwa illustration
- stylized not photorealistic
- youthful late-teen face proportions
- soft cel shading
- semi-flat clean colors
- simplified school or study-room background
- mobile-readable vertical webtoon composition

## style_negative
- no photorealism
- no live-action look
- no skin pores
- no painterly skin texture
- no realistic hair strands
- no glossy idol styling
- no mature adult facial structure
- no western comic style
- no 3D render

## tone
- romance as emotional blackmail
- school pressure, loneliness, pride, score volatility
- each panel readable on mobile
- every 3~4 panels should escalate tension
